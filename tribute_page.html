Atal Bihari Vajpayee

Former Prime Minister of INDIA

Timeline of Atalji's life

  • 25 December 1924 : Atal Bihari Vajpayee was born in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh
  • 1939 : Joined the Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) and became its full-time worker (technically a "Pracharak") in 1947.
  • 1942 : He was arrested for 23 days, along with his elder brother Prem, for participating in Quit India Movement and was released on the condition (in written) that he would never participate in any anti-British struggle.
  • 1951 : Joined Bharatiya Jana Sangh (a newly formed Hindu right-wing political party) and was appointed its National Secretary of the northern region.
  • 1957 : Atalji was elected to theLok Sabha in the Second General Elections for the first time from the Balrampur constituency in Uttar Pradesh
  • 1968 : Became the National President of Jana Sangh.
  • 1977 : 30 years of Congress rule was ended, and a new government was formed at the centre. In this new government, Atal Bihari became the External Affairs Minister.
  • 1977 : Atalji become is the first person to address the United Nation in Hindi.
  • 1978 : US President- Jimmy Carter visit to India, when Atalji was the External Affairs Minister of India.
  • 1980 : Founded Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) along with his colleagues and became its first National President.
  • 6 December 1992 : He was highly criticized for inciting the mob for the demolition of "Babri Masjid" in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh.
  • 16 May 1996 : He became the 10th Prime Minister of India.
  • 19 March 1998 to 22 May 2004 : Again served as the Prime Minister of India.
  • 13 May 1998 : He put India in the league of the World's elite nuclear club after the successful nuclear test at Pokhran in Rajasthan named- Operation Shakti.
  • 19 February 1999 : He took a bus journey to Lahore in Pakistan in order to build strong relations with Pakistan.
  • 23 December 2002 : While inaugurating the first section of the Metro in Delhi, he also had bought a ticket for himself to travel in the Metro train. He also became the first passenger of the Delhi Metro.
  • 2005 : Retirement from all political activities.
  • 16 August 2018 : Atal Bihari Vajpayee was died in All India Institute Of Medical Sciences New Delhi, New Delhi.

Awards Won by Atal Bihari Vajpayee

  • 1992 : The Padma Vibhushan.
  • 1993 : Kanpur University honoured him with D.Litt.
  • 1994 : He was bestowed with the Bharat Ratna Pandit Govind Ballabh Pant Award.
  • 1994 : Best Parliamentarian Award.
  • 1994 : Lokmanya Tilak Award.
  • 13 February 1999 : Grand Cordon of the Order of Ouissam Alaouite by the Government of Morocco.
  • 2015 : He was honoured with India's highest civilian award - the Bharat Ratna.
  • 7 June 2015 : He was conferred Bangladesh's Liberation War Honour by the Government of Bangladesh.
